# Whitepaper Corpus

## Reading Map

| Cluster | Whitepapers | Why Start Here |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| Protocol core | Main, Smart Cash, Uniqueness, Use Cases | Establishes the privacy-first settlement model and core product claim |
| Privacy and security | Privacy Threat Model, OnionNet, PQ Migration, Linked Liability | Defines privacy quality, network help, migration risk, and fraud boundaries |
| Rights and asset models | Assets/Rights/Vouchers, Cross-Chain Integration | Expands the object family beyond simple cash |
| Governance and economics | DAO, Legal Architecture, Tokenomics, Proof-of-Useful-Work | Sets control, treasury, and incentive boundaries |
| Expansion paths | Agentic Offline Economy, Cross-Chain Integration, Use Cases | Shows where the same settlement model grows next |
| Strategy and terminology | Litepaper, Competitors Research Report, Marketing Strategy, Corpus Terminology Reference | Compresses the public category, launch discipline, and shared vocabulary |
