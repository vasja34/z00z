# Governance And Economics

> The governance, legal, and token papers are all trying to answer the same question: how can Z00Z coordinate upgrades and incentives without becoming an operator.

## Control Boundary

The DAO and legal papers both insist on bounded authority, explicit challenge
windows, and proof-of-non-control. Governance is necessary, but it must not
quietly recreate a managed treasury or service surface.

## Economic Boundary

The tokenomics and PoUW papers keep the native asset important for fees, bonds,
budgeting, and useful-work rewards, while still avoiding a story where every
external economy must become a foundation-approved token lane.

The native-asset image stub preserves provenance for the visual material around
native asset roles, budget compartments, and tokenomics surfaces.

## Public Claim Governance

The marketing strategy turns the legal and governance posture into a release
discipline for public language. Major public artifacts should preserve neutral
protocol, independent issuer, scoped disclosure, and checkpoint-finality
boundaries; they should avoid official-service, risk-free bridge, and instant
finality phrasing.
