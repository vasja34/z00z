<objective>
Close the remaining implementation-grade gap around primary crash recovery after
quorum but before DA publication. Specify the exact certificate-bound
publication-resume contract, the recovered-primary rejoin policy, and the
minimal runtime, store, and scenario changes required to reject detached resume
or implicit failback while keeping planner truth deterministic and local.

- promotion back to primary MUST happen only at a checkpoint boundary or placement or route generation activation boundary
- planner truth MUST remain deterministic replicated config; this addendum MUST NOT introduce planner leader election, planner failover, or planner state ownership
