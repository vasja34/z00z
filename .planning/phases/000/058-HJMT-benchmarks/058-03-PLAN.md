placement, planner mode, journal paths, workload profile, failover schedule,
aggregator or shard cardinality, join mode, transfer target, publication leaf
set size, and route-generation activation behavior. Add or freeze exact
acceptance homes for import/export roundtrip and tamper reject coverage over
`ShardRouteTableV1`, `ShardRootLeafV1`, `CheckpointPublicationV1`,
`BatchProofBlobV1`, and journal checkpoint manifests. Prove startup fails
closed on wrong route digest, stale-route state, wrong journal lineage, wrong
codec bytes, unsupported root or backend generation, wrong proof-family tag,
and corrupted persisted state. Keep to-disk or from-disk restart, import or
export roundtrip, and corrupted import reject as explicit evidence rows rather
than implied by generic restart coverage. Keep config-surface coverage,
journal-baseline and WAL-boundary coverage, restart and persistence coverage,
`test_hjmt_batch_commit.rs`, and `test_hjmt_batch_recovery.rs` on this same
operational-readiness slice as exact live homes. Preserve the RedB-backed local
journal as the
active baseline and treat any ordered WAL or replicated-log direction as a
future `JournalBackend` adapter subject to conformance and equal-durability
gates rather than as a second active runtime layer.

  real artifacts to the wrong verification contract.
