This UAT session covers the Phase 061 wallet-tree flattening and rename
closeout as a regression pass on the shipped canonical paths.

Because Phase 061 is primarily structural, the checklist is intentionally
behavioral and release-oriented: cold start, wallet open or reopen, RPC,
key or seed paths, tx or backup flows, and final closeout guardrails must all
still hold on one canonical wallet tree.
