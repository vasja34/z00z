<action>
Flatten the remaining semantic-heavy wallet domains to one-level files. This
includes the tx tree, claim helpers and verifier shards, stealth support,
wallet-layer files, backup subtrees, and the small chain subtree. Update
claim-helper and tx-helper names exactly as the TODO table requires, keep the
semantic boundary between wallet state and external history untouched, execute
the stealth `zkpack` move against the live `src/stealth/zkpack.rs` plus
`src/stealth/test_zkpack.rs` contract rather than the stale TODO
`facade_zkpack` rows after `061-01` reconciles that drift,
update `tx/claim_tx.rs`, `tx/mod.rs`, and the included verifier shards so
digest, hash, and nonce references use `claim_tx_hashing` while statement or
proof helpers use `claim_tx_statement`, and move wallet and backup docs into
the canonical `crates/z00z_wallets/docs/` area so they no longer live under
`src/`. Preserve the live persistence story from the referenced wallet docs
throughout the move.
</action>
