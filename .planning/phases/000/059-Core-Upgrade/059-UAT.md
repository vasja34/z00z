### 1. Asset Cash Projection Remains Clean

expected: Wallet balance, asset RPC, and simulator transfer flows show spendable
cash only from Assets. Voucher and Right inventory must appear on separate
projections and must never inflate cash balance.

- `crates/z00z_wallets/README.md`

- `crates/z00z_wallets/src/adapters/rpc/methods/object.rs`
- `crates/z00z_simulator/tests/test_scenario1_object_flows.rs`
pass_when:
- wallet object inventory lists Asset, Voucher, and Right on one inventory
  facade;
- `wallet.asset.*` rejects voucher/right ids;
- `wallet.object.*` exposes voucher/right lifecycle without changing cash
  balance;

- a voucher appears in spendable cash;

### 2. Voucher Offer, Acceptance, Reject, And Transfer Safety

expected: Voucher flows require explicit object handling rather than asset send
semantics. Offer, accept, reject, and transfer paths must stay bounded by the
voucher policy and holder/beneficiary split.
evidence:
- `crates/z00z_wallets/src/adapters/rpc/methods/object_impl.rs`
- `crates/z00z_storage/tests/test_store_api.rs`
- `crates/z00z_simulator/src/scenario_1/scenario_config.yaml`
- `crates/z00z_simulator/tests/test_scenario1_object_flows.rs`
pass_when:
- policy-allowed transfer changes holder without silently rewriting beneficiary
  or refund authority;
- reject/refund flows use voucher lifecycle paths rather than asset rollback;
- simulator packet anchors and emitted evidence include `voucher_flow.json`,
  `val_flow.json`, and `watch_flow.json`, with `voucher_flow.json` preserved in
  inventory as a canonical `pending_exact_home` public anchor.
fail_when:
- voucher transfer is routed through `wallet.asset.*`;
- reject/refund behaves like arbitrary clawback;
- non-transferable voucher transfer is accepted.
result: [passed]

consumed backing, and residual voucher state. Partial redeem must preserve a
validator-checkable residual claim or an explicit terminal closure.
evidence:
- `crates/z00z_storage/tests/test_store_api.rs`
- `crates/z00z_wallets/src/adapters/rpc/methods/object_impl.rs`
- `crates/z00z_simulator/tests/test_scenario1_object_flows.rs`
pass_when:
- full redeem converts conditional value into final asset outputs only on an
  accepted validator path;
- partial redeem records redeemed amount and residual voucher amount explicitly;
- unchanged or invalid roots are rejected for replay or double redeem attempts.
fail_when:
- residual amount drifts from the redemption arithmetic;
- voucher is redeemed twice;
- wallet-side `RedeemPartial` mapping disagrees with runtime/storage semantics.
result: [passed]
