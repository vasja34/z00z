<action>
Implement D-03 by adding `SettlementPath`, `TerminalId`, and `SettlementLeaf` with family-tagged canonical encoding for `Asset(AssetLeaf)` and `Right(RightLeaf)`. Add `RightLeaf` fields for version, terminal id, right class, issuer scope, provider scope when relevant, holder/control commitment, beneficiary commitment when relevant, payload commitment, expiry or validity window, one-time-use marker when relevant, revocation or transition policy, challenge window, disclosure policy id, and retention policy id. Add `serde(deny_unknown_fields)` or the project-equivalent strict decoder at serialized boundaries, hash-domain separation between asset and right leaves, strict right path/leaf consistency checks, a narrow right-class taxonomy for machine, agent, human, and enforceable non-coin rights, and transition validation for create, transfer, consume, expire, revoke, and challenge flows. Reject right leaves containing fee budget, payer, sponsor, relay, or processing-support semantics. Use project serialization abstractions and typed errors, not ad hoc `serde_json` or public panics.
</action>
