# 057-05 Plan: Validator, Watcher, Scope-Continuity, And Scenario Sync

<objective>
Bind downstream acceptance and scenario evidence to one canonical publication
contract.

Purpose: prove that validator acceptance, watcher export, first-seen scope
continuity, and simulator lineage evidence all consume the same publication
truth without creating a second registry, second verdict story, or detached
scenario narrative.
Output: validator and watcher digest sameness, dynamic-scope publication
continuity, and scenario-sync closure on the checked-in runtime packet.
</objective>
