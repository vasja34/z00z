<objective>

root-of-shard-roots evidence.

without collapsing shard-local HJMT proof truth or corrupting historical
compatibility across root-generation and route-generation changes.
Output: layered proof-family contracts, compatibility guards, and historical
proof vectors that fail closed on illegal continuity claims.
