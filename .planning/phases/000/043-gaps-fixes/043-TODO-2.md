## 🎯 Decision Summary

The execution baseline for Phase 043-2 is:

1. keep the public verifier honest and require an explicit witness before any public conservation claim;
1. split manual asset-class auditing into typed target, status, outcome, and mismatch classes instead of collapsing failures into one error;
1. keep the encrypted forensic archive transport seam intact, but make the wallet-prefixed JSONL history file canonical and required;
1. keep the wallet snapshot file, canonical JSONL history file, and live tx-history directory colocated and stem-synced;
1. keep `outputs/tx_exports` as a separate summary export tree;
1. make forensic enablement explicit through constructor or import-mode selection only.
